The Upside and Downside of Mental Shortcuts

Biases work as mental shortcuts.

Do you think biases can both work in your favor or at times block you?

Regardless of whether you clicked Yes or No biases are two sides of the same coin. Biases help us cut corners in our thinking processes to help save time and effort in making judgments. Often they work well, but sometimes, they block us from receiving new information or insights.
