Why Might We Develop Biases?

See why we need our biases? Researchers tell us we make 40,000 decisions every day!

Biases are judgements or assumptions about a person, place or thing that is often based on past experience.

Our biases help us rely on what we think we know, or on the judgement of others who we like or trust.
