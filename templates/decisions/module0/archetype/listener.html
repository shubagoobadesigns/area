Why is Mahatma Gandhi a listener? Mahatma Gandhi was able to change India through non-violence because of his ability to listen to others and understand the needs of his people and their arguments. In turn his listening enabled him to be persuasive, able to reason and negotiate between conflicting parties, whether it was Muslims and Hindus, or between the British government and its Indian subjects.
