Decision Dilemmas - Explanation

Why?

There is not always a clear relationship between how easy a question may seem and whether we are confident that we know how to solve it. If we hate our hair then what to do with our hair may seem easy but we may be unsure that we know how to make it look good!

There is also a relationship between knowing how to tackle a problem and whether or not you have a process or system.
