Critical Concepts are the one, two or three things that really define your decision's success.

Nylah's three answers clarifying her vision of success are her Critical Concepts.

  1. I'm able to afford it.
  2. I want to study graphic design.
  3. I still want to help out at home.

Your Critical Concepts may be similar-or they may be different. They will reflect what matters to you personally.
