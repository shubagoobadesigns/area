To make big decisions better starting at the end can help you make sure that you are solving for what really matters to you in the outcome.

By thinking about what you need for the decision to succeed before you solve it you know where you need to end up-and you can now tell the story of your success.

AREA calls these your CRITICAL CONCEPTS.

Critical Concepts are the one, two or three things that really define your decision's success.

Nylah's three answers clarifying her vision of success are her Critical Concepts. {% include 'decisions/module1/nylah_cc.html' %}

Your Critical Concepts may be similar-or they may be different. They will reflect what matters to you personally.
