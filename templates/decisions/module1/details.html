  • Introduction
  • The thing about decision making is that there's no right answer to the questions.
  • HOWEVER, there is likely a right answer for YOU.
  • Life has easy and difficult questions and we each have a different idea of what is easy and difficult for us.
  • In fact we might have the very same decision - say where to live - be easy at some point in our lives and yet difficult at another point in time.
  • So how can you solve problems in a way that's both COMFORTABLE for us and where we have CONFIDENCE that we're making decisions that help us reach our goals?
