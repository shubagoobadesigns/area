One Vision of Success to determine whether you should go away to college might be:

  1. I can afford it
  2. College has a good program in my area of interest
  3. Someone else can take care of my younger siblings

These 3 success factors make up what are called Critical Concepts.

Critical Concepts are the one, two or three things that need to happen in the outcome of your decision for you to know that the decision has succeeded for you personally.
