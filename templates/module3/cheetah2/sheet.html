All high-stakes decisions have numbers involved. Focusing on numbers has two benefits:

  1. It enables you to see your Target as objectively and uninfluenced as possible.
  2. It also allows the Target to present data on what it considers to be its main issues.

Time to do your first research! Pause the module here. Go to the Internet and search your Target’s website(s) for numbers relevant to your decision. We’ll start a timer that signals you to come back to the module in 10 minutes to keep going on your problem solving.

If you’re deciding about buying a home or leasing more workspace, useful numerical data would be the cost of the homes/spaces in your target location and property taxes.
